SECTION 4: OPERATION 4 Fundamentals (Handling
Characteristics)
Understanding machine handling is critical for safe operation.

   Both **forklifts** and **telehandlers** handle differently from road vehicles.
   For **forklifts**, **rear-wheel steering** causes the back to swing wide during turns.
   For **telehandlers**, **boom movement** constantly shifts the **center of gravity**.
   Operators must develop an instinct for machine response under varying load, terrain, and speed.
   Subsequent cards will detail specific handling scenarios.
