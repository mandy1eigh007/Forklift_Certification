Introduction
Safe powered industrial truck operation relies on five foundational principles:

     Stability, visibility, capacity, positioning, and awareness.
     Apply across all equipment classes, including forklifts and telehandlers.
     Protect operators, coworkers, and property.
     Core concepts remain constant, despite specific equipment application differences.

The five official principles of safe operation from the Genie Lift Pro Telehandler Trainer's
Guide are:

1.   Avoid hazardous situations
2. Always perform a pre-operation inspection
3. Always perform function tests prior to use
4. Inspect the workplace
5. Only use the machine as it was intended
     Operators are responsible for learning and practicing these principles.
     Refer to the training manual and machine's operator's manual for details.
     Each principle will be discussed in depth.
